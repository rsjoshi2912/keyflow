Features

Everything you need to build speed

A focused practice tool — no ads, no accounts, no cloud. Just you and the keyboard.

Timed typing tests

30 seconds to 10 minutes, plus passage mode. Live WPM and accuracy as you type, with a smooth caret and error highlighting.

Honest scoring

Net & gross WPM, keystroke accuracy, error count and a consistency score — corrections don’t erase your mistakes.

10-step lesson curriculum

From home row to full-alphabet words. Each lesson unlocks at ≥20 WPM and ≥90% accuracy, so fundamentals stick.

Progress dashboard

Speed trend chart, recent sessions, personal bests and a daily streak to keep the habit going.

Offline-first & private

No account, no telemetry, no cloud. Your results are stored locally on your device — and never leave it.

Dark & light themes

The same warm amber-on-slate look you see on this page — in dark or light, and your settings persist across restarts.

Scoring

Numbers you can trust

KeyFlow uses standard, exam-grade formulas — the same math is unit-tested in the engine.

Net WPM

72 wpm

Correct characters ÷ 5 ÷ minutes. Only correctly typed words (and their spaces) count.

Accuracy

96.4 %

Correct keystrokes ÷ total keystrokes. Fixing a typo doesn’t erase the mistake.

Consistency

88 /100

100 minus the variation of your per-second speed. Steady beats bursty.

Gross WPM

78 wpm

All typed characters ÷ 5 ÷ minutes — your raw speed before errors.
