04 — FAQ

Before you download.

Is VType really free?

VType is free to download and use: timed speed tests, typing games and your progress dashboard, with no ads. The full 16-step lesson course and the exam mock tests are part of an optional Pro upgrade. No account is needed to start — sign in free to sync your progress across every device.

Can I practice SSC, CPCT or RRB typing exams?

Yes. Mock tests are modeled on SSC CHSL/CGL DEST, CPCT English typing, RRB NTPC skill test and court patterns. They replicate the real rules — backspace disabled, continuous passage, fixed duration — and give a clear qualified / not-qualified verdict. Always verify the latest official notification for exact rules.

Do I need an account? What syncs?

You sign in once with an email and password or with Google — it's free, with no ads or paid tiers. Your sessions, streaks, lesson progress and personal bests then sync securely to every device you sign in on. Typing itself still works offline; your results sync the next time you're online.

Is sign-in with Google supported?

Yes. Choose Continue with Google on the login or register screen — on the site and in the desktop app — to create your account in one tap. You can also use a regular email and password, and reset it any time from the Forgot password link.

Why does my computer warn me on first launch?

The builds aren't code-signed yet, so macOS Gatekeeper and Windows SmartScreen show a one-time warning for new developers. On macOS, right-click the app → Open. On Windows, click More info → Run anyway. You can verify your download against the published SHA-256 checksums.

I used KeyFlow — is this the same app?

Yes. KeyFlow was renamed VType with version 1.0.0. Your existing progress migrates automatically the first time you open VType on the same computer.
